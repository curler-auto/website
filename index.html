The Reality of Scaling QA

Are you stuck with these?

As engineering teams grow, quality becomes the bottleneck. Sound familiar?

Flaky, unreliable tests?

Tests that pass one run and fail the next erode trust, block merges, and bury real bugs under noise.

Slow, painful regression?

Regression cycles that take days delay every release and force teams to choose between speed and confidence.

Brittle CI/CD pipelines?

Pipelines that break on every change demand constant babysitting instead of shipping value.

Rising QA maintenance costs?

Every new feature multiplies test maintenance, quietly draining engineering hours and budget.

No performance visibility?

Shipping without performance benchmarking means latency and load issues surface in production, not before.

Slipping release velocity?

When QA can't keep pace with development, release confidence drops and roadmaps slip.
